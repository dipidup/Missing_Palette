Dumb, Short and Creative

I’m Ankit Agrawal

Hey! That creepy face, there on the right isn't real :p
I am Ankit and I own very little. Apart from my Engineering degree from BIT Mesra-Ranchi, I own a few sets of colors, stationery & gadgets. Long trips, books, old music, and desi chai are a few things that fascinate me. With some guidance and a lot of practice, I developed a little artist inside me who is trying to learn in every possible way. My passion for art developed during the school days and it turned into my 'Go to Place'. During my engineering days, I was fortunate enough to collaborate with a few artists who helped me strengthen my love for colors.
I have conducted more than 15 art workshops across the nation and whenever I get time, I love to spend a part of it with a canvas and some colors. Although I like working with all the mediums but watercolor is something which I love handling the most. I always try to keep a travel sketchbook and a set of watercolors whenever I travel.
I have a few exhibitions and art projects under my hood and love to explore various angles of Art.
Yes, I am a techie & am currently a part of RnD space at an MNC. I spend a good amount of my time learning new technology every day. Ping me, if you know about an old song or a place that serves some decent tea. Looking forward to a few good days.

Missing Palette?

Well, I used to have a lovely small color palette which I used for a few of my initial oil paintings. Unfortunately, I am dumb and obviously ruined the palette by letting the colors dry over it.
Also, 'Ankit' is such a common name that I had to rename myself :p
