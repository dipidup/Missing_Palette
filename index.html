Dumb, Short and Creative

I’m Ankit Agrawal

Hello there! That quirky face may seem a bit eerie, but rest assured, it's just for fun!
I'm Ankit, a proud owner of a few prized possessions: an Engineering degree from BIT Mesra-Ranchi, a collection of vibrant colors, stationery essentials, and a handful of gadgets. Life's simple pleasures, such as embarking on extensive journeys, immersing myself in books, listening to timeless melodies, and savoring a cup of desi chai, never fail to captivate my imagination.
During my school days, I discovered my deep-rooted passion for art, which eventually became my refuge. Throughout my engineering journey, I had the incredible opportunity to collaborate with talented artists who further fueled my adoration for the world of colors.
Over the years, I've organized multiple art workshops & exhibitions. While I appreciate working with various artistic mediums, watercolors hold a special place in my heart. I make it a habit to carry a travel sketchbook and a set of watercolors wherever I travel. I've had the privilege of showcasing my art through exhibitions and participating in diverse art projects. Alongside my artistic endeavors, I embrace my role as a full-time management consultant in an MNC.
If you happen to come across an old song that resonates deeply or stumble upon a hidden gem of a place that serves remarkable tea, don't hesitate to reach out to me.

Missing Palette?
