About

Hello! I'm Ankit, an Engineer with a passion for art. Life's simple pleasures journeys, books, old melodies, and desi chai captivate me. My love for art started in school and grew during my engineering years, collaborating with talented artists. I’ve organized workshops and exhibitions, with watercolors being my favorite medium. Whether traveling or working as a full-time management consultant, I always carry my sketchbook and paints. If you ever find a beautiful old song or a great tea spot, feel free to reach out!
