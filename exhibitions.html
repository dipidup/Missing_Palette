01 - Exhibitions

Showing work in public spaces.

Group shows, solo exhibitions, and gallery appearances - from Ranchi to national platforms.

Campus Feature
2018
Exhibition at Bitotsav - BIT Mesra, Ranchi

Exhibited works during Bitotsav, the annual cultural festival at Birla Institute of Technology, Mesra. Showcased watercolor paintings capturing campus life, everyday moments, and figurative studies.

2019
Banaras Plein-Air - Group Exhibition

Watercolors painted on the ghats of Banaras, shown as part of a group exhibition. Three works from the Banarasi dawn, dusk, and midday series.

2022
Portrait Studies - Solo Show

A selection of oil and watercolor portraits - figures from everyday India, painted from life and from memory. Shown in Ranchi.

2018
BIT Mesra Campus Exhibition

Works shown during the annual cultural festival at Birla Institute of Technology, Mesra. Featured watercolors of the campus and figurative studies.

2021
Emerging Artists - Group Show, Pune

Selected for a group show of emerging artists in Pune featuring acrylic and oil works. Confusion and Hayaa among the exhibited pieces.

04 - Workshops

Teaching in classrooms & village schools.

From DAV Group schools to a government programme in rural Jharkhand - art instruction across settings.

2021
Saansad Adarsh Gram Yojana - Mahilong Village, Jharkhand

Conducted a painting workshop for children at a government primary school in Mahilong village under the Saansad Adarsh Gram Yojana programme. The session introduced watercolour basics and observational drawing to students who had never held a paintbrush.

2023
D.A.V. Group of Schools - Jharkhand

Workshop series across D.A.V. schools, endorsed by Dr. T.P. Pati (Former Regional Director). "The workshop witnessed huge participation. Students loved the sessions."

2018
Sri Agrasen School, Bhurkunda

"Children's hidden talent revealed." - Mr. Pravin Rajgadia, Director. A sketch and watercolour workshop for school students, with coverage in regional press.
